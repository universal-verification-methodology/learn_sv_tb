# Examples — SVA implication timeline

Track A prompts. Browser lab **`sva-timeline`** is shipped.

## Prompts

1. Restate the core idea of **SVA implication timeline** in one sentence.
2. In your own words, capture this beat: *You have built directed testbenches, classes, constraints, and coverpoints. Assertions are the next piece: they state what must stay true across clock cycles, not just what one test vector checked. An implication…*
3. Draw `a |-> b` vs `a |=> b` on a 4-cycle timeline with one overlapping pass.
4. Give one vacuous-pass example (antecedent never true).
