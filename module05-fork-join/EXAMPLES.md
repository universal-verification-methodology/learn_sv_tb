# Examples — Fork / join

Track A prompts. Browser lab **`fork-join`** is shipped.

## Prompts

1. Restate the core idea of **Fork / join** in one sentence.
2. In your own words, capture this beat: *When a testbench needs parallel activity — two stimulus threads, a monitor and a driver, or a timeout watchdog — you fork separate threads and then choose how the parent waits. Join waits until every child finishes.…*
3. Draw fork/join vs join_any vs join_none for clock + stimulus threads.
4. Name one deadlock you can create with join and a forever clock.
