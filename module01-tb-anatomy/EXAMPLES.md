# Examples — TB anatomy

Track A prompts. Browser lab **`tb-anatomy`** is shipped.

## Prompts

1. Restate the core idea of **TB anatomy** in one sentence.
2. In your own words, capture this beat: *A SystemVerilog testbench is a simulation-only wrapper around a design under test. The TB drives inputs, instantiates the DUT, observes outputs, and ends the run. Classic pieces: stimulus nets you assign in initial…*
3. Label DUT, TB, and which side drives each port on a tiny and2 sketch.
4. Write a `$display` then `$finish` outline for a one-vector smoke.
