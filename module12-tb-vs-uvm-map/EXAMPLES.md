# Examples — TB vs UVM map

Track A prompts. Browser lab **`tb-vs-uvm-map`** is shipped.

## Prompts

1. Restate the core idea of **TB vs UVM map** in one sentence.
2. In your own words, capture this beat: *You have written flat directed testbenches—clock and reset, tasks, fork-join, classes, constraints, cover, and assertions. UVM reorganizes the same jobs under different names. Pin wiggle becomes a driver behind a…*
3. Map six classic TB roles to UVM components (test, seq, driver, monitor, agent, scoreboard).
4. Say what stays the same when you move from directed TB to UVM.
